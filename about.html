About One Tek Solution

Built for serious electronics sourcing, design and manufacturing.

One Tek Solution is an R&D based company, trading partner and manufacturer of electronic products. We support OEMs, EMS companies, industrial buyers and engineering teams with components, design and manufacturing support.

Company Profile

We work across four platforms: R&D, Sales, Import and Manufacturing. Our focus is practical electronics engineering, reliable semiconductor sourcing and customized product development for automotive, railway, industrial power and safety applications.

We deal in all types of ICs, microcontrollers, RF devices, sensors, power devices, MOSFETs, diodes, regulators, memory, passive components and obsolete or hard-to-find components.
