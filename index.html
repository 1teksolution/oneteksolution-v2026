About One Tek Solution

One Tek Solution is an R&D based electronics company as well as a trading, import and manufacturing partner for electronic components and custom electronic products. We help OEMs, EMS companies, system integrators and engineering teams with reliable sourcing, BOM support and electronics product development.

R&D Division

PCB design, embedded firmware, IoT design, product development and custom electronics as per customer requirement.

Import Division

Global sourcing for hard-to-find, obsolete, allocation-phase and regular semiconductor components.

Manufacturing Division

Automotive flashers, mini inverters, SMPS, chargers, fire alarm systems and railway anti-theft systems.
